Cindy's Lunch Special

Description

Inspired by Whole Life Challenge's article, "How to Make 5 Healthy Lunches in 50 Minutes", this recipe combines your favorite vegatables, chicken and recipe to create an easy meal for the week.

Ingredients

Makes 4 servings

  • 3 bell peppers
  • 2 roma tomatoes
  • 2 zucchini squash
  • 1 stalk of celery
  • 1 pound of Chicken Breast
  • 1 cup of brown rice
  • Oil
  • Seasonings, such as salt, pepper, chicken seasoning, lemon pepper, anything that feels right

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Dice or chop the bell peppers, roma tomatoes, zucchini squash and celery.
  3. Combine all the vegetables in a bowl. Mix with 1 TBSP of oil, 1/2 tsp of salt, and 1/2 tsp of pepper. You can also add 1 tsp of lemon pepper for more zest!
  4. Chop the chicken into cubes or strips.
  5. Line a baking dish or pan with foil. Coat or spray with oil, place chicken onto pan. Season chicken with 1 TBSP of oil, salt, pepper, and chicken seasoning. The amount varies based on how seasoned you enjoy your food.
  6. Now combine the vegetables onto the baking pan with the chicken.
  7. Place dish into oven for at least 40 minutes. Move aorund the chicken after the first 20 minutes.
  8. In the meantime, boil water to make your brown rice. Follow the instructions on your rice packagae label.
  9. When both dish and rice are done, you have your meals ready! Combine the rice and chicken-veggie mix into bowls or containers. Enjoy!
